MISSION

Ka-Patir-An Business in San Joaquin, Pasig City, is on a mission with its Pater Meals initiative. Our aim goes beyond just offering delicious, culturally rich meals; it's about empowering local chefs and food entrepreneurs. Through the promotion of Pater meals flavors and the incorporation of sustainable practices. Our goal is to uplift the community's economic development while preserving its culinary traditions. The Pater Meals, we aspire to nurture a vibrant food culture that instills a deep sense of community pride, bringing residents together through the shared joy of exceptional, locally inspired cuisine.

VISION

Our vision encompasses the development of a sustainable and thriving ecosystem of businesses, with a particular focus on nurturing local entrepreneurship. We aspire to see a community that celebrates its unique cultural heritage through initiatives like Pater Meals, where traditional flavors intertwine with modern culinary innovation. By fostering economic growth, social equity, and environmental responsibility, we envision Ka-Patir-An Business playing a pivotal role in creating a vibrant and interconnected community that serves as a model for sustainable development and cultural preservation.
